Purpose

The purpose of the conference is to present some of the latest mathematical advances for contemporary risks such as climate risk, cyber threats, extreme events, and new financial challenges. By bringing together research centers from diverse countries, we aim to provide a high-quality platform for knowledge sharing and fostering collaboration among academics. A half-day will be dedicated for practitioners’ talks.
